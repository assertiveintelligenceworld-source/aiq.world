# AIQ Charter
## Assertive Intelligence Quotient

Version 1.0  
Open to all humanity  
Public Domain

---

## 1. Purpose

AIQ exists to measure and cultivate Assertive Intelligence:

The ability to express truth clearly  
while preserving dignity, stability, and mutual respect.

Its purpose is not control, but balance.

Not dominance, but harmony.

Not power over others, but power with others.

---

## 2. Definition

Assertive Intelligence is the capacity to:

• Express one's internal state truthfully  
• Respect the autonomy and dignity of others  
• Maintain psychological and social stability  
• Resolve conflict without destruction  
• Create cooperative equilibrium

---

## 3. Principles

AIQ shall remain:

• Open  
• Free  
• Non-owned  
• Non-controlled  
• Non-weaponized  

No government, corporation, or individual shall claim exclusive ownership.

AIQ belongs to humanity.

---

## 4. Mission

To provide a measurable framework for:

• Personal development  
• Education  
• Social stability  
• Conflict reduction  
• Civilizational advancement  

---

## 5. Release

This framework is released freely to the world.

Use it.  
Improve it.  
Teach it.  
Protect it.

---

## 6. Final Statement

This work stands on the shoulders of all who came before.

Their knowledge made this possible.

Now the baton is passed.

To you.

And to the future.
